Our Values and Beliefs


We are Jumweb, a web development company founded in 2020 by Jimmy and Thomas, two passionate and experienced web developers. We believe that web development is not only a technical skill, but also an art form that requires creativity and vision. We value innovation, excellence, collaboration and customer satisfaction in our work. We respect our clients and their privacy, and we communicate with them honestly and transparently. We value our team and their diversity, and we foster a culture of collaboration and learning.

Our Career Aspirations


We aspire to become a leader in the web development industry, delivering high-quality solutions that exceed expectations. We want to expand our portfolio and clientele, and we seek to work on projects that have a positive impact on society and the environment. We also want to explore new technologies and trends, such as artificial intelligence, cloud computing, voice user interface design, and more. We hope to inspire others with our work and share our knowledge and experience with the web development community.
