Our History

We are Jumweb, a web development company founded in 2020 by Jimmy and Thomas, two passionate and experienced web developers. Our mission is to create beautiful, functional and secure websites for our clients, using the latest technologies and best practices. Our vision is to become a leading web development company in the industry, delivering high-quality solutions that exceed expectations.
